Yam and Egg Sauce

Description

Yam and egg sauce is an ubiquitious meal eaten for breakfast in Nigeria. It is really easy to make and takes very minimal time to prepare.

I'll breakdown the processes required to make this really delicious meal.

Ingridents

  • Yams
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh Peppers
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasonigs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Peel the yams and wash them.
  2. Place the yams in a pot of boiling water and cook for about 30 mintes.
  3. Dice your tomatoes, fresh peppers, and onions.
  4. Place your pan on the stove and heat the oil
  5. Place the diced tomatoes,onions and peppers and fry
  6. Add salt and seasoning to the frying mixture
  7. Break your eggs and whisk , then pour into the pan with the tomatoes,onions and peppers
  8. Fry until the eggs are golden.
  9. Remove from the fire after it's done and allow to cool.
  10. Serve and enjoy. Delicious!
